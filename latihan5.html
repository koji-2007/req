About Oriflame
Oriflame is a leading beauty company selling direct. We are present in more than 60 countries, of which we are the market leader in more than half. Oriflame has its origin in Sweden with own production units in Sweden, Poland, China, Russia and India. We offer a wide range of high-quality beauty products as well as a unique opportunity to join our sales force and start your own business.
